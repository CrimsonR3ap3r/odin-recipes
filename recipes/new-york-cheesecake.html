New York Cheesecake

Description:

A New York style vanilla cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 15 graham crackers, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter together in a meduim bowl until well combined; press onto the bottom of the prepared pan to make a packed crust.
  3. Mix cream cheese and sugar together in a large bowl until smooth. Blend in milk, then mix in eggs, one at a time, until just combined. Add sour cream, flour, and vanilla; mix untill smooth. Pour filling onto the prepared crust.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 1 hour. Turn the oven off; let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door closed for 5 to 6 hours to prevent cracking.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
