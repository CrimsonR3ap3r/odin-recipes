Chicken taco lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 bonless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4 strips
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • 1 ounce taco seasoning mix
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • Salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 8 ounce package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste
